  Texbook and prerequisites
We will primarily use the following textbook which is available for free online. I will provide links to additional reading material, if any, in the class calendar below.
  • Networks, Crowds, and Markets: Reasoning About a Highly Connected World (Cambridge University Press, 2010) by David Easley and Jon Kleinberg
CSE 241 (Algorithms and Data Structures) is a nominal requirement for this course, but all that you really need is familiarity with high-school algebra. Some prior exposure to probability and statistics will help, although I will try to review these topics briefly as and when required.
